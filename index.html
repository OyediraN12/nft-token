Why Should I Choose FameDoge?

ameDoge is a famous token with a large and active community.Built on Binance Smart Chain network.Ownership of FameDoge has been waived.This means that a specific change to the FameDoge contract isn’t possible forever. FameDoge believes it’s more effective for the community to have a strong marketing vault than to offer short-term and small amounts of ‘BNB’ or ‘BUSD’.An ideal tax of 5% for buying and selling is automatically sent to the marketing wallet. Buying and selling greatly strengthens the liquidity and marketing wallet.2% of every buy and sell transaction is directly added to the liquidity. This means constant growth. FameDoge’s main goals are to become more popular through marketing, sponsorship and collaborations. FameDoge’s deflation solution will be provided in two ways.The first and most important of these is to take a FameDoge from the pool and burn it forever, and the second is to organize award-winning burning events. Also,half of the NFT revenues will be used for the FameDoge token burning.In this way, FameDoge will become more difficult to reach and more known over time.

FameDoge NFT Strategy

he NFT world has been trending for everyone lately.FameDoge is committed to acting with innovative ideas to expand the community. NFT cards can be one of the main features that distinguishes FameDoge from others.It will be for the benefit of the community that the reward from NFTs is distributed to community members and used in the burning of FameDoge. FameDoge NFT cards will be minted on the Solana or Kadena network thanks to its low transaction fees and fast transfer feature.Solana has been mentioned with NFT projects recently and still continues.
